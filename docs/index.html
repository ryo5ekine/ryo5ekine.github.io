In the Town of Mervetrim

TRAILER

YouTube

The shop, tucked away in a corner of an alley,
was the only tie-cutter in town.

It was a boy all alone, who ran the place with no ties of his own.

Today, another customer came.
It was a pair who never looked each other in the eye.
The boy cut the threads that constrained them together

“Enough is enough,” they said.

“Cut the bond between my daughter and her father,”

Today, another customer came.
It was the woman’s daughter hiding in the corridor.
It was the woman’s daughter hiding in the corridor.
She quietly held out a string for the boy.

“This one,” she whispered.

And so, the boy cut the bond between the daughter and her mother.

Today, another customer came.
It was a beautiful boy who would captivate anyone he passed.
The boy cut the silk threads which sewed his feet down.

“I’m afraid of those gazes,” he said.

Today, another customer came to cut their ties.
A customer, yet again, comes to have their ties cut —
